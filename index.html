What Happen to Ronaldo?

Portuguese forward Cristiano Ronaldo joined Manchester United for a second time in September 2021 when he moved back to Old Trafford from Juventus. His first stint saw him evolve from a raw teenage winger signed for £18m from Sporting Lisbon into one of the world's greatest players, winning multiple league titles and the Champions League with the Reds. His United return was cut short following a controversial interview with Piers Morgan in which he criticised the club, and he now plays for Al-Nassr in the Saudi Pro League.
